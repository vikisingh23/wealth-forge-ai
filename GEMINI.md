# WealthForge AI

Personal Finance Agentic AI Team for Indian and US financial optimization.

## Agent Instructions
- Core architecture principles and agent workflows are defined in [AGENTS.md](./AGENTS.md).
- Domain-specific logic for India and US is maintained in the `agents/` directory.

## Financial Rules & Standards
- All advice must comply with the [Financial Disclaimer](./rules/FINANCIAL_DISCLAIMER.md).
- Strict data privacy and PII masking rules are defined in [DATA_PRIVACY.md](./rules/DATA_PRIVACY.md).
- Reusable financial logic is modularized in the `skills/` directory.

## Configuration
- MCP servers for market data and financial calculators are configured in `.mcp.json`.
